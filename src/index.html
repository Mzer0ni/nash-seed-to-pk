Nash seed words to private keys

For demonstration- and emergency use only. This tool is passing your seed phrase through the same wallet-creation-steps, used by Nash Protocol during your account setup, and spits out the unencrypted keys. Everybody with your private key has unrestricted access to your wallets. Be vigilant while using with live wallets.
